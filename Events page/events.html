Community Events

Highlighting 6 of the most popular yearly community events in the North Conway, NH area to plan with your group while staying at The Darby Field Inn.

Hannes Schneider Meister Cup (March)

Celebrate the "Father of Modern Skiing" with a spirited weekend of amateur racing, vintage alpine fashion, and slopeside festivities at Cranmore Mountain. This premier event blends ski history with high-energy competition, making it a must-visit for winter sports enthusiasts.

Seek the Peak Weekend / Expo (July)

Join New England’s ultimate hiking festival, where outdoor adventurers gather to conquer Mount Washington and celebrate with a massive gear expo and after-party. Whether you’re hitting the trails or browsing the latest mountain tech, it’s a weekend of pure peak-bagging energy.

Mt. Washington Bicycle Hillclimb (August)

Experience the thrill of "The Toughest Hillclimb in the World" as elite cyclists battle the legendary Auto Road for a shot at mountain glory. Even if you aren't riding, the summit views and the sheer determination of the athletes make for an unforgettable spectator experience.

Fall Festival (September)

Kick off the harvest season with a charming celebration of New Hampshire life, featuring local artisans, family-friendly games, and seasonal treats. It’s the perfect way to soak up the crisp mountain air and the very first hints of autumn color.

Fryeburg Fair (October)

Step into Maine’s largest agricultural fair for a quintessential New England experience filled with world-class livestock shows, thrilling carnival rides, and legendary fair food. Located just minutes from North Conway, this massive autumn tradition is a highlight of the season for visitors of all ages.

Peak Foliage & Busker Fest (October)

Marvel at the world-famous White Mountain colors while the streets of North Conway come alive with the eclectic sounds and talents of professional street performers. This vibrant weekend pairs breathtaking natural beauty with a lively, festival-style atmosphere right in the heart of the village.
