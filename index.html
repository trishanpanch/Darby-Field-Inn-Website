The Concept

Not a hotel.
A Home.

For generations, The Darby Field Inn has stood watch over the White Mountains. We have reimagined this historic property as a tranquil, rustic getaway for many to enjoy.

What we offer: a peaceful home away from home. We provide a no-frills, cozy environment perfect for outdoorsy groups. The 13 unique rooms are reserved solely for your party.

Step right out the front door to go on hikes, and return to the comfort of nature, helping create lasting memories for all who can enjoy.
